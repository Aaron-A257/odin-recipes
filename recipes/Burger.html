Burger Recipe

Description

The Perfect Basic Burger Recipe uses ground beef and an easy bread crumb mixture. Top it with your favourite condiments!


Ingredients Required:

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.

  2. Step 2

    Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.

  3. Step 3

    Add ground beef and bread crumbs; mix with your hands or a fork until well blended.

  4. Step 4

    Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.

  5. Step 5

    Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.

  6. Step 6

    Serve hot and enjoy!
